Make It Spatial

Because Location Matters

Welcome! Make It Spatial is a learning hub and blogspot for anyone interested in geospatial analytics and GIS. Currently, the website is in development, but eventually, it will be home to a variety of tutorials, lessons, and blogs about the challenges I encounter in my work.

I am a geospatial data scientist and current PhD student at NC State University. My research typically takes place at the intersection of energy, transportation, and land use change. That said, I have also worked on projects related to hydrology, environmental health, and energy economics. If you are interested in working together to solve a pressing research problem, feel free to reach out here.
