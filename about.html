About

The site and the creator

Welcome! Make It Spatial is a learning hub and educational blog for anyone interested in geospatial analytics and GIS. Currently, the website is in development, but eventually, it will be home to a variety of tutorials, lessons, and blogs about the challenges I encounter in my work.

I am a geospatial data scientist and current PhD student at NC State University. My research typically takes place at the intersection of energy, transportation, and land use change. That said, my work often connects with a wide variety of disciplines!
